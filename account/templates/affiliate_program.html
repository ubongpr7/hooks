Affiliate Program Policy

1. Introduction

The HooksMaster.io Affiliate Program allows you to earn a 30% recurring commission on customer subscriptions generated through your unique referral link.

2. Eligibility and Enrollment

Affiliates must complete an application and agree to these terms. Membership in the affiliate program is at the discretion of HooksMaster.io and may be revoked for violations of these terms.

3. Commission Structure

  • Payout Rate: Affiliates earn a 30% commission on recurring customer payments.
  • Payout Schedule: Commissions are paid monthly, provided a minimum payout threshold is reached.

4. Affiliate Responsibilities

Affiliates agree to promote HooksMaster.io responsibly, following ethical guidelines, and avoiding misleading claims or unauthorized branding use.

5. Termination of Affiliate Status

Affiliates may be removed from the program for violating these terms or misusing affiliate marketing guidelines. Upon termination, no further commissions will be earned.
